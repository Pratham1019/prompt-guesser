# Product

## Register

product

## Users
AI enthusiasts, gamers, and general web users looking for a fun challenge. They want to test their intuition and learn how generative AI models interpret text, competing to guess the prompts that produced high-quality AI images.

## Product Purpose
An interactive prompt-guessing game. The product exists to make prompt reverse-engineering fun and educational, training users on prompt keywords (artists, style terms, lighting, composition) through game-like mechanics and visual feedback loops.

## Brand Personality
Playful, dynamic, and visually engaging. The voice is friendly, encouraging, and has the high-energy feel of a modern digital game.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic corporate SaaS dashboards (no dull gray grids, no plain cards, no sterile charts, no corporate blue palettes). Avoid unstyled browser forms or plain text-based layouts.

## Design Principles
1. **Visual Centricity**: The generated image is the hero. All panels, inputs, and feedback elements must draw the user's eyes back to the image being guessed.
2. **Rewarding Feedback Loops**: Every guess (correct, close, or incorrect) should trigger satisfying feedback. Use micro-animations, score trackers, and styling changes to celebrate progress.
3. **Painless Guidance**: Use progressive disclosure for hints and clues (such as revealing prompt length, character counts, or word categories) to prevent player frustration.
4. **Clean Contrast & Motion Controls**: Ensure accessible contrast for all texts and respect the system preference for reduced motion while keeping the core experience alive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG AA compliance**: Body text must maintain a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 against its background. Saturated fills will use high-contrast white text.
- **Clear focus indicators**: All interactive elements (inputs, buttons) will feature highly visible focus rings.
- **Respect Motion Preferences**: All animations must respect `prefers-reduced-motion` settings.
